OUR STORY

Built by athletes, for athletes. We believe every workout deserves the best equipment.

THE BEGINNING

VORTEX started in a small garage in Venice Beach, California. As fitness enthusiasts, we were tired of shaker bottles that leaked, didn't mix properly, or fell apart after a few months.

We set out to create the perfect shaker bottle—one that actually works and looks great doing it. After hundreds of prototypes and thousands of hours of testing, VORTEX was born.
